Andres' Cryptocurrencies inherintance scheme
============================================

The process consists of regenerating a wallet seed based on chunks of data sent to two diferent groups of people.

The chunk to reconstruct the seed is in the .rar file. You need the password provided earliear by Andres to access the data.

With the data, you can get together with any of these contacts of the other group who will have the other part needed to put together the seed:
	# contact 1
	# contact ...
	# contact N

After having 2 out of 3 parts, you should use the Shamir.html file to put together the parts and reconstruct the seed.

Use Trust Wallet to enter the reconstructed seed and be able to move/cash out the crypto assets:
	https://trustwallet.com/download
